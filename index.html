Our Mission

At Pacific Pediatric Dentistry, Dr. Sun and his staff believe that healthy smiles play an integral role in developing happy and healthy kids. We make it our priority to care for your child in a fun, comfortable, and individualized manner. We are committed to providing optimum, long-term dental care for infants, children, adolescents, and persons with special needs.

We understand that children need to feel comfortable in a dental office, and our office is specifically designed to do just that! We have an open bay treatment area, kid-friendly decor, a play area stocked with toys and games, and TV screens above each operatory. In addition, we approach every child and family with respect, explaining each step of each child's dental care in age-appropriate terms.

We warmly welcome you to our office, and look forward to becoming your child's dental home!

Meet the Dentist

Benjamin Sun - pediatric dentist

Dr. Benjamin Sun is a proud native of Seattle, Washington. He earned his Bachelor of Science at the University of Washington and his dental degree at the University of Southern California. Although he has always loved being around kids, Dr. Sun discovered his passion for working with children after traveling to Mexico, Belize, and Guatemala on several dental mission trips. This led him to pursue specialized training in pediatric dentistry.
